They discovered Kella Darkhope a Zhentarim spy hiding in the tavern. Then reaching the keep they prepared for an incoming attack from the Zhentarim force.     The adventurers make their way to the Dripping Caves to rescue the villages. Upon leaving the town, they encounter a hoard of orca led by a Mogunar, the half orc tavern brawler of daggerford. With the help of the clumsy Felgolos, they managed to annihilate the hoard leaving only a few orcs including Mogunar alive. They made a new friend which went flying off towards the coast.

    With that they continued to the caves where they where ambushed in the front cavern. Following the tracks of the villagers, they managed to find and save them. Now they are taking a moment to talk to Morak Ur'gray the Nightstone barkeep who seems to have taken the leader position.

    Heading to the dripping caves tve heroes survive an initial ambush and find the villagers. They manage to safely bring them all back and all seems good until they're informed  villagers are missing. Going back in they meet Snigbat, a goblin that considers herself queen as claims to have a few followers. They mend forces to defeat Hark, and kill the goblin boss despite efforts to be civilized. Queen Snigbat orders them to rake care of the Blob, an ooze that's been restricting water acces to the goblins.

    The adventurers leave with promise of killing the slime in exchange for discover more of the tunnels. Back in Nighstone they help clean up and scavenge what is left before heading to sleep.
